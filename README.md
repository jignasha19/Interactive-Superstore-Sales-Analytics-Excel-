# Sales Analysis Insights

* **No or low discounts** maximize profit; high discounts (>20%) reduce profitability.
* **Technology category** drives high margins despite lower volumes; Office Supplies provide steady revenue.
* **Top revenue states**: California, New York, Texas; West & East regions dominate overall sales.
* **Standard shipping** is the preferred mode; First Class offers premium upsell potential.
* **Seasonality observed**: peak sales in Nov–Dec, slowest in Feb & June; steady year-on-year growth.
* **Top-selling products** contribute majority of revenue → prioritize inventory and promotions.
